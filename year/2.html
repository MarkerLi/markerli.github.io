School Life

Year 2

In my second year, I have given myself a several goals. The first goal was to join a competition. I have joined Jackie Chan Table Tennis and I have helped my hostel to win the first place of that year. The experience was great as I had brilliant teammates in my hostel and they taught me how to practise and to improve my skills.

The second goal was to join an Integrated-Learning programme (ILP). I can do numerous sports like distance running, swimming, playing table tennis but I do not know how to play tennis. So, I decided to join the tennis course. It was quite difficult to me as tennis was a new aspect for me in sports. The coach was nice in which he usually gave us more time to practise. Well, I can conclude that tennis is really not suitable for me after completing the course. XD

My second year in university has broaden my horizon that I did not have before. There were lots of fruitful experiences and they were really unforgettable.
