School Life

Year 1

As a starter in year 1, I have encountered many difficulties. The first obstacle I have faced is that the living environment here was totally new. Apart from the living environment, everything in here were new too like the teaching systems, the pace of living every day and most importantly I had to blend in the social circles in a bid to meet new friends.

Well, my first year was a little bit harsh as I did not know what to do in first year. I was confused with everything. However, I have met some VETS through LCE courses and they were really nice to me. I have talked to them sometimes and they have guided me a lot. Therefore, my first year in Lingnan University was quite great actually.
