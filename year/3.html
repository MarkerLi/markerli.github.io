School Life

Year 3

Third year of my university life was essential to me as I need to think about my future prospects. This year was also a terrible year for all of us.

As we know, we have lots of social unrests in Hong Kong and classes were suspended for several months. It then changed to online lessons. It was a whole system to all of us as we have never used this system before. The teaching methods, mid-terms, group projects, presentations and even exam were completely different and I have taken some time for me to catch up the teaching progress.

Till now, a new disease from China Wu Han has gone virus and it is fatal. Everyone in Hong Kong are afraid of this new disease and we all wear masks while we are outside for the sake of our lives. Lingnan University has declared that face-to-face lessons are suspended until further notice. Well, I wish everyone is safe.

Apart from the above two events, this year is quite normal to me as I stay at home everyday in order to keep myself alive. I hope the virus will be gone soon.
