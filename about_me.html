About Me

Hi, this is me, Mark. Nice to meet you!

Well, to begin with, it is such a pleasure to study in Lingnan University. Although Lingnan University is far away from home, I still love this university as it brings me many new things and experiences. For example, there is a hostel life in here and it is the first time I have hostel experiences.

University is a place for students to grasp more high-leveled knowledge as well as a way to improve your life skills. In my point of view, university is a place for people with same sights and hobbies to gather together. Not only we can make new friends in here no matter local or not, but also we can develop our relationship network here for future purposes.

Anyway, I am a BBA student who is majoring in Risk and Insurance Management. There is one year left for me to graduate and I am going to enjoy my final year!
