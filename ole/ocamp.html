Other Learning Experience

Orientation Camp

Every undergraduate wants to join orientation camp (O Camp) so much and I am not excluded. I have joined my hostel’s O Camp when I was a year 1 undergraduate. It lasted for 3 days and 2 nights. It was really fun and unforgettable; I have never been so excited before as we have played many games like mass game in Jackie Chan Gymnasium, detective game, play games with water on beach and the like. It was really thrilling and breathe-taking throughout those three days.

After 2 years since I was year 1, I joined O Camp again. This time, my role was to be the freshmen’s mentor. It was my first time to guide year 1 undergraduates and it was quite challenging to me.The role of being mentor was more difficult than I thought. Luckily, other group mates have helped me throughout the O Camp. It was really unforgettable.
