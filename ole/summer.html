Other Learning Experience

Summer Term

In summer, I have registered summer courses in order to catch up my academic progress. I wish I could finish my courses as soon as possible so I have registered one course in each summer semester.

Summer term only have four weeks to study as it is a concentrated course for students. Mostly, we need to attend two to three days per week and three hours per day. The advantage of summer term is that we can focus on that single course only throughout a month; the drawback of it is obviously the heavy workload.

It is still part of my university life and I think summer terms still enriched my university life. After all, it is still part of my study!
